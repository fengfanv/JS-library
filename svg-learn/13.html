Bell Let’s have another example with cubic and quadratic beziers. Here the bottom of this bell is defined with straight lines. Then a quadratic Beziers starts the bell cloak. Then the line is continued with a cubic Bezier to form the top of the bell. Then we reach the bottom part with another quadratic bezier.

贝尔 让我们再举一个三次和二次贝塞尔曲线的例子。在这里，这个钟的底部是用直线定义的。然后一个二次Beziers开始钟形斗篷。然后，这条线用三次贝塞尔曲线继续，形成钟形物的顶部。然后我们用另一个二次贝塞尔曲线到达底部。
