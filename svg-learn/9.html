Christmas bauble with gradient The filling of a shape can be defined as a gradient. Here we apply a radial gradient to our Christmas decoration to have a subtle 3D effect. It has a different syntax than CSS but the capabilities are rather similar. Gradients in SVG though can also be applied for strokes and pretty cool effects can be achieved that way.

渐变圣诞饰品 形状的填充可以定义为梯度。在这里，我们将径向渐变应用于圣诞节装饰，以获得微妙的3D效果。它的语法与CSS不同，但功能非常相似。SVG中的渐变也可以应用于笔划，这样可以实现非常酷的效果。
